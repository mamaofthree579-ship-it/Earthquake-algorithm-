# Data sources & endpoints (examples)

- USGS Earthquakes (GeoJSON feeds): https://earthquake.usgs.gov/earthquakes/feed/v1.0/summary/all_week.geojson
- IRIS / IRIS DMC (seismic): https://ds.iris.edu/
- Smithsonian GVP (volcanoes): https://volcano.si.edu/
- NOAA NDBC (buoys): https://www.ndbc.noaa.gov/
- NOAA SWPC (solar wind & space weather): https://services.swpc.noaa.gov/
- JPL HORIZONS API: https://ssd-api.jpl.nasa.gov/horizons.html
- Minor Planet Center (MPC): https://www.minorplanetcenter.net/
- ECMWF APIs & reanalysis (may require registration)
